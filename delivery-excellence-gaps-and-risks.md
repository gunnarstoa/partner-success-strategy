# Delivery Excellence — Opportunities to Strengthen (Future Consideration)

Companion to [delivery-excellence-deep-dive.md](delivery-excellence-deep-dive.html). These are areas
to build out next to make the plan even stronger. Parked for later, out of scope for the current
pass.

The supply-and-demand capacity work that started here has since moved into the plan as §10 (Partner
Capacity & Resource Coverage).

## Highest priority
- Define the support paths. Add how we step in when a partner needs more help at the gate, when a
  project would benefit from extra support mid-flight, and how a partner can repeat a ladder stage
  to build more confidence. This rounds out the model beyond the main path.
- Plan PS capacity and funding. The model invests PS effort up front — pre-sales scoping, the
  first-project lead, weekly cadences — and that investment pays back as the ladder builds partner
  independence. Decide how PS is staffed for the early load and how pre-sales work is funded ahead
  of close.
- Design the partner incentives. Make "assume selected" attractive to partners: deal routing,
  co-marketing, and ladder status as a selling point, alongside credentialing as the path to
  delivery.

## Important
- Add a client-readiness view. Alongside partner readiness, track what the client brings — SME
  availability, decision speed, executive sponsorship, and appetite for change — so both sides are
  set up to succeed.
- Define ownership (RACI). Make clear who makes the gate decision, who signs off the handoff, and
  who runs the launch review, including the PSM's role across the lifecycle.
- Keep credentials current. Add a recertification cadence so credentials stay current as apps
  release new versions. This is the "continuous capability uplift" idea from the original slide.

## Smaller items
- Add program-level measures. Show how the program is doing overall — delivery quality trending up,
  PS hours per project trending down, and how partners are distributed across ladder stages.
- Connect the pillars. Link the co-selling specialist from Pillar 02 to the Pillar 03 delivery team
  for continuity.
- Extend to multiple partners and tiers. Cover GSI plus boutique combinations, subcontracting, and
  flexing the gate by partner tier.
- Agree how we assess scope changes with partners, so mid-build discoveries are handled
  consistently and fairly.
